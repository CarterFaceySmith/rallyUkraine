  • RETURN ALIVE UKRAINE

    • REGIONS AVAILABLE: WORLDWIDE
    CHARITY SCORE: N/A
    TAX DEDUCTIBLE: NO

    CHARITY MAIN FOCUS:
    Purchasing of military supplies, distribution of aid supplies

    SUMMARY:
    In 2014, an IT specialist from the Ukrainian capital of Kyiv, Vitaliy Deynega, began buying bulletproof vests to donate to Ukrainian soldiers defending territory from Russia, on each he inscribed the words 'Come Back Alive'.

    Eight years on and now a full non-government Ukrainian organisation, Come Back Alive assists Ukrainian soldiers primarily through the purchasing of equipment via donations/crowdfunding, alongside offering technical, psychological and medical support.

  • SAVE THE CHILDREN

    • REGIONS AVAILABLE: WORLDWIDE
    CHARITY SCORE: 4/4
    TAX DEDUCTIBLE: NORMALLY

    CHARITY MAIN FOCUS:
    Distribution of aid resources to civilians, securing of safe passage for refugees

    SUMMARY:
    Save The Children is an Australian nonprofit organisation focused on providing humanitarian aid to those most vulnerable in times of crisis, children.

    Founded in England in 1919, Save The Children has gone on to assist in numerous relief efforts worldwide, and is now focusing on providing emergency items to Ukrainians alongside working with neighbouring countries to ensure safe passage for those fleeing Ukraine.
